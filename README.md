# Microsoft-Elevate-Power-BI
# AI Adoption Impact Analysis Across Industries

## Project Overview

This project analyzes the adoption of Artificial Intelligence (AI) across various industries and evaluates its impact on business performance indicators such as revenue growth, market share, and consumer trust.

The project uses an interactive dashboard built in Power BI to visualize and interpret industry-level AI adoption trends.

## Tools Used

* Power BI
* Microsoft Excel
* Data Visualization Techniques

## Dataset Features

The dataset includes the following attributes:

* Industry
* Country
* Year
* AI Adoption Rate
* Revenue Increase Due to AI
* Market Share of AI Companies
* Consumer Trust in AI
* Job Loss Due to AI
* Human–AI Collaboration

## Dashboard Insights

The Power BI dashboard provides insights such as:

* AI adoption rate across industries
* Revenue increase associated with AI usage
* Market share distribution of AI-driven companies
* Comparison of AI adoption across countries

## Conclusion

The project demonstrates how AI adoption is influencing industry performance and highlights sectors where AI implementation has the greatest impact.
